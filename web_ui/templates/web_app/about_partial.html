ACI Config Converter

Having to migrate basic configurations (such as SNMP, DNS, etc) to a new environment can be repetitive and error prone. Specially if you are migrating your infrastructure to a new environment. With this solution you will be able to configure your APIC controller from your current standalone nexus switches with just a few clicks.
